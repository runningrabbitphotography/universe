Your Solar System

Your Solar System is where you live. It is made up of a Star we call the Sun, various Planets that orbit around the Sun and any of the Moons that a Planet may have. The Planet Earth is where you live. Lastly, there is the Asteroid Belt, made up of, what else, but Asteroids!! Asteroids come in many sizes, but are mainly made of rock and are smaller than Planets.

Your Galaxies

Your Solar System is part of a Galaxy. The Galaxy you live in is called the Milky Way. A Galaxy contains many, many stars, each star of which usually has it's own solar system and associated planets and moons.

A Galaxy is located amongst other Galaxies and make up what is called a Galaxy Cluster. Galaxy Clusters are located amongst other Galaxy Clusters and make up what are called Galaxy Superclusters.
