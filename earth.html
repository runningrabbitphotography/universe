Earth

Earth is the Third planet from the Sun in Your Solar System. It is the only planet we know of that has living things, notably Humans. There are more than 8 billion humans on the planet. Earth is known as the Blue planet, mainly because of all the liquid water on its surface. Earth is the biggest of the planets closest to the Sun. Earth has only one moon.
