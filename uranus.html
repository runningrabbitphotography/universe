Uranus

Uranus is the Seventh planet from the Sun in Your Solar System. It was the first planet found with the aid of a telescope in 1781. It was named after the Greek God of the sky, Uranus. Uranus is an ice giant and has thirteen known rings. Uranus has twenty-seven moons.
