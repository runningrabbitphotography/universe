Galaxies

Think of a neighborhood. A neighborhood with lots and lots of houses. Each house has a family, with parents and children. A Galaxy is the neighborhood. Each house is Your Solar System with a star and its associated planetary system. Except that a Galaxy has billions and billions of stars. Which means there are even more billions of planets. The Galaxy that Your Solar System lives in is called the Milky Way.

A Galaxy likes to live around other galaxies, just like there are many neighborhoods in a city. These are called a Galaxy Cluster. A Galaxy Cluster likes to live around other Galaxy Clusters. Just like there are many cities in a state or country. These are called Galaxy Superclusters.
