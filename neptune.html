Neptune

Neptune is the Eighth planet from the Sun in Your Solar System. It is an ice giant with high winds and at least five main rings. Neptune is a very dark and cold planet. It is the only planet not visible to the naked eye. Neptune has fourteen moons, the most noticeable of which is Triton.
