The Sun

The Sun is really a Star at the center of Your Solar System. It is the brightest object in the Earth's sky. The Sun is very, very old and very, very hot. Somewhere around 4.6 billion years old. The energy of the Sun is the source of sunlight and warmth that the Earth and you rely on. The birth of your Sun was the result of much larger Stars running out of fuel and exploding. Around this same time, Your Solar System also formed.
